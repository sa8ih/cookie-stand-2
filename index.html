What are salmon cookies?

Salmon cookies are the great new treat that's built out of two Seattle traditions - salmon and coffee-time eatery. With fresh-caught Alaskan salmon ground into the dough of every cookie, it's a way to enjoy a little sample of our iconic Pacific Northwest cuisine in a small, delicious confection. Swing by one of our five locations and try it yourself!
